Contact Us

We train at the Centennial Gymnasium at the Taieri College in Mosgiel. You are welcome to come and have a watch us train to see if it is for you. The gymnasium is easily located by driving down the short driveway on Church St.

Class Day Time
Main class (10+) Monday & Wednesday 6:00pm – 7:30pm
Mini-kids (under 10) Wednesday 5:30pm – 6:30pm

You can also find us on Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/MosgielTaekwonDo. We have a closed group for Students and Parents, please send a request to join.

You can also email us at enquiries.mosgieltkd@gmail.com or text/call Mr. Bob Brown on 021 066 4660. We look forward to your message!
